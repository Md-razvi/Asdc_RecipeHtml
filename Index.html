Chicken Fried Rice

Click here for Detail Instructions

Ingredients for the Recipie

  • Boneless Chicken cut into 1” thin strips- 250 gms.
  • Cooked Rice (day old)- 6 cups (made from 2 cups or 350 gms of raw rice)
  • Eggs- 3
  • Garlic, fine sliced- 6 cloves
  • Salt- 1/4 tsp and a pinch for seasoning
  • Pepper powder- 1/2 tsp
  • Dark Soy sauce - 3 tsp
  • White Vinegar- 1 tsp
  • Shredded cabbage- 1 cup
  • Carrots, julienned- 1 cup
  • Green peppers (capsicum) julienned- 1 cup
  • Spring onion, greens- 3 tbsp
  • Refined oil- 3 tbsp

Preperations

  • Wash and pat dry the boneless chicken pieces and cut into 1” wide flattish strips. Set aside.
  • Shred the cabbage into thin slices and julienne the carrots and the green peppers.
  • Fine slice the garlic cloves and cut the spring onion greens into small pieces.
  • Beat 3 eggs in a bowl.

Process:

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok and add the beaten eggs. Add a pinch of salt to season, mix gently and then scramble it on medium heat. This should take a minute. Remove onto a plate and set aside.
  2. Clean the wok with a paper towel and add 2 tbsp oil.
  3. Add the slices of garlic. Give a stir and fry it on high heat for around 30 secs.
  4. Add the chicken strips and stir fry on high heat for 1 min till they turn white.
  5. Now add the salt & pepper powder, mix and stir fry for around 3 mins on high heat till the strips are browned.
  6. Add 1 tsp soy sauce, mix and stir fry the chicken for 2 mins on medium heat.
  7. Add the julienned carrots and peppers, mix and stir fry for 2 min.
  8. Now add the shredded cabbage and continue to stir fry for another 1 min on high heat.
  9. Add the rice and mix with the chicken and veggies.
  10. Season with a pinch of salt and 1/4 tsp pepper powder. Give it a mix.
  11. Add 1 tsp of white vinegar and 2 tsp of dark soy sauce. Toss and fry 2 mins on medium heat.
  12. Add the scrambled egg and the spring onion greens.
  13. Toss well and serve.
